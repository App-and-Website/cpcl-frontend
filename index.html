About Centre for Puja and Culture Ltd (CPCL)

Founded in 2003, Centre for Puja & Culture Ltd (CPCL) was inspired by the Bangladesh Society for Puja & Culture Inc (BSPC) to create a spiritual and cultural sanctuary. CPCL aims to provide facilities for worship, cultural festivals, education, and Hindu rituals. It serves as a platform for the small community of Bangladeshi Hindus residing in Sydney and its surroundingareas. CPCL celebrates Bengali language, art, and philosophy, runs community welfare programs, supports migrants and students, and fosters harmony in Australia’s multicultural society.
